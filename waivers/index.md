## Important Notes

- **Review Carefully** - Please read the entire waiver before signing
- **Ask Questions** - If you have questions about any terms, contact us before signing
- **Original Signatures** - We require original, signed waivers (or digital signature via email)
- **Minors** - Parents/guardians must sign waivers for participants under 18
- **Legal Counsel** - Consider having an attorney review if this is a commercial event
- **Keep Copies** - Retain a copy for your records
