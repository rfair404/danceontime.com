## Lesson Structure

1. **Warm-up** - Get loose and review previous material
2. **New Material** - Learn steps, timing, and styling
3. **Choreography** - Put it all together with music
4. **Practice** - Refine and build confidence
5. **Cool-down** - Review key points for home practice

## What to Expect

- **Personalized Attention** - Every couple is unique; lessons are tailored to your pace and goals
- **Clear Instructions** - Both lead and follow positions are taught equally
- **Musical Selection** - We work with your music choice or help you select the perfect song
- **Confidence Building** - Our goal is for you to feel amazing on your special day
