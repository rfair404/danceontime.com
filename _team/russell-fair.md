Russell Fair is the founder and lead instructor at Dance On Time. With years of professional dance experience and multiple regional competition awards, Russell specializes in wedding choreography, couples instruction, and creating unforgettable dance experiences for celebrations of all sizes.

His expertise spans Country Two Step, Waltz, Swing, Salsa, and Bachata, and he's dedicated to helping clients feel confident and joyful on their special day.
