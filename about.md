## Our Mission

We're dedicated to creating unforgettable dance experiences. Whether you're taking your first dance lesson or hosting your dream event, we bring expertise, energy, and passion to every moment.

## Meet Your Instructor

Our dance instructor is a **regionally-competing dancer** with multiple awards and years of professional experience. Specializing in wedding choreography and couples instruction, they bring both technical skill and personalized coaching to every lesson.

### Expertise & Certifications

- Regional competition winner (multiple awards)
- Specialized in: Country Two Step, Waltz, Swing, Salsa, Bachata
- Professional experience in wedding instruction, couples coaching, and group choreography
- In-studio and in-home instruction available

## Why Choose Dance On Time?

**For Your Wedding**: We've choreographed first dances that brought guests to tears and got the whole room dancing. Our packages are designed specifically for couples preparing for their big day.

**For Your Event**: Our DJ and dance floor services work seamlessly with our instruction—your guests will be impressed by the complete package.

**For Your Peace of Mind**: We handle every detail, from choreography to music to venue setup, so you can focus on celebrating.
